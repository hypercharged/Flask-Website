About me





Welcome to Hypercharged!


Hypercharged was founded in January of 2017 by a bunch of highschool friends focusing on something they all loved: cars. The group first began with YouTube videos about car meets they attended which can still be found today on YouTube (linked below). But eventually, they expanded to Instagram as well. On Instagram they began posting screenshots from videos to gain more traffic to their videos but eventually branched out from just videos to photography too. Now, Hypercharged has over 5 team members with a focus on photography and videography. We hope you join us for our crazy ride as we continue to expand and do some crazy cool projects!
